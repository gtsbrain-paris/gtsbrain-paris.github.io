Job openings

We are always looking for candidates with a strong background in ML to join our team. Here is a list of 3 profiles for potential candidates:

  • Intern: a Master's student (although exceptional Bachelor's students are welcome to apply) in ML/CS, preferably with prior internships in ML.
  • Fixed-term (<3 years) research engineer: a person holding a Master's or Ph.D. degree in ML with proven experience closely related to teams' publications.
  • Permanent research engineer: a person holding a Ph.D. degree in ML with at least 1 publication as a first author in a top-tier ML conference. Strong experience in LLMs, foundation models or physics-informed ML models is preferred.

Feel free to contact Ievgen Redko by email or on Linkedin if you would like to apply.
